Welcome to the $SCI2WEB server site at the $PROJ[SCI2WEBSITE].  This is a mirror site of the Sci2Web home site.

In this site you will find publicly served several scientific applications developed and adapted by researchers in our institution and research group.

$SCI2WEB is a web suite intended to provide simple but powerful web interfaces to what are called Naturally Developed Scientific Applications (NDSAs).  NDSAs are software products developed or adapted by scientists as part of their own research and that can be useful to many other scientist inside or even outside their field.  Since NDSAs are primarily developed to solve specific scientific problems, they normally lack a proper user interface and quite often low level programming skills could be required to get, compile and use them.  

$SCI2WEB allows scientists to make publicly available their software even to users without the skills or the computer power to run their codes.  You will find In this server site several scientific applications ported to a $SCI2WEB environment and ready to be ran in our servers.

In this site you will find:

  • Documentation about $SCI2WEB and other related links to this on-line scientific applications web suite.
  • General information about our institution and research group.
  • Access to applications served in the $PROJ[SCI2WEBSITE].
